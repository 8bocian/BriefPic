BriefPic Privacy Policy

Introduction

BriefPic is a mobile application that allows users to take pictures of book pages and create notes out of them. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your personal information safe. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use BriefPic.

Camera Usage

BriefPic requires access to your device's camera in order to take pictures of book pages. When you use BriefPic for the first time, you will be prompted to grant permission for the app to access your camera. If you do not grant this permission, you will not be able to use BriefPic's camera feature.

We only allow you to take pictures of book pages that you have the right to access and use. You are solely responsible for the pictures that you take using BriefPic's camera feature, and we do not collect or store any of your pictures on our servers. All pictures taken using BriefPic are stored on your device and are not accessible by us or any third party.
